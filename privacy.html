Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 8, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Lerno ("we", "us") processes information when you use the Lerno mobile app and related services (collectively, the "Service").

1. Information We Collect

We may process the following categories of information:

  • Account information: email address, username/login, and profile name (if provided).
  • User content: materials you upload or create in the Service (for example: PDFs, images, text notes, generated quizzes, and related metadata).
  • Study and usage data: learning activity such as streaks, completed sessions, answers, and statistics.
  • Device and diagnostics: device model, OS version, app version, crash logs, and basic diagnostics needed to improve reliability and security.
  • Network data: IP address and request metadata for security, abuse prevention, and troubleshooting.
  • Subscription information: subscription status/entitlements as provided by the App Store. We do not receive or store your full payment card details.

2. How We Use Information

We use information to:

  • Provide and operate the Service (accounts, sync, content processing, and generation).
  • Process uploaded materials for OCR and AI generation as requested by you.
  • Maintain security, prevent abuse, and enforce limits and fair use rules.
  • Provide customer support and respond to your requests.
  • Improve performance, reliability, and user experience.

3. Sharing and Processing by Third Parties

To provide core functionality, we may share limited data with service providers acting as processors, such as:

  • Infrastructure providers (hosting, storage, databases, email delivery, and monitoring).
  • OCR providers to extract text from your uploaded materials.
  • AI/LLM providers to generate learning content based on the materials you submit.

We do not sell your personal information. We may disclose information if required by law or to protect our rights, users, or the Service.

4. Data Retention

We retain information as needed to provide the Service. Uploaded materials may be stored for a limited time depending on your subscription plan and the Service settings (for example, "source storage" retention). We may retain certain logs for security and fraud prevention.

5. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures designed to protect information (for example, encryption in transit and access controls). No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Your Choices and Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, export, or delete certain information. You can request account deletion or data access by contacting us.

7. Children

The Service is not intended for children under 13 (or the minimum age required in your jurisdiction). If you believe a child provided us personal information, contact us so we can take appropriate action.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will update the "Last updated" date and, if changes are material, provide additional notice as required.

9. Contact

For privacy questions, contact us at privacy@lerno.space.
